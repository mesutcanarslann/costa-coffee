How to order for delivery

Place your order with our partners

Download or visit the Uber Eats, Deliveroo or Just Eat app or websites to get started.

Search for Costa Coffee and browse

Set your location and choose your favourites from the available menu.

Sit back and relax. Your order is on the way

Our baristas will prepare your order in store ready for contact-free pick-up and delivery.
